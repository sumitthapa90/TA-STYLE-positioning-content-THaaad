INTRODUCTION

Skullcandy Inc. is an American company based in Park City, Utah that markets headphones, earphones, hands free devices,audio backpacks, MP3 players, and other products.Skullcandy's products are targeted at the outdoor action sports demographic (snowboarders, skateboarders, etc.) and general consumer market.Skullcandy products are sold through retailers, specialty outlets, corporate incentive programs and the company's online store.
